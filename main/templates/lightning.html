Staff of Lightning

Step 1:

The first step is to aquire the purple musical disk which is located in the around Generator 4.

Step 2:

Ride the tank and jump off at each platform in order to collect all three parts of the staff. You will have to take the tank around the map at least twice to get all of the parts.

Step 3:

You will now have to get the Lightning Crystal. This is located in the "Crazy Place". You must take the music player to the tunnel located at Generator 5 and place it down to open the portal to the crazy place. Walk through the portal and take the Lightning Crystal from the podium. You can only carry one crystal at a time so you must now leave the "Crazy Place" and construct the staff.

Step 4:

Head to the centre of the map where the "Pack a Punch" is located and head down the stairwell where you picked up the music player. Go to the podium for the Lightning Staff and construct your staff.
