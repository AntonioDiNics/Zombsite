Staff of Ice

Step 1:

The first step is to aquire the blue musical disk which is located in the room to the right of Generator 2.

Step 2:

Next, you have to wait until a round where it is snowing. You have to collect three different parts that make up the staff. To get each part, you will have to dig at various different dig sites accross the map using a shovel that you picked up in the starting room. The first part can be dug up at any dig site located in the first section of the map - "The Trenches". The second part can be dug up at any dig site located in the second section of the map - "No Man's Land." The third part can be dug up at any dig site located in the third section of the map - "Church".

Step 3:

You will now have to get the Ice Crystal. This is located in the "Crazy Place". You must take the music player to the tunnel located at "Church" and place it down to open the portal to the crazy place. Walk through the portal and take the Ice Crystal from the podium. You can only carry one crystal at a time so you must now leave the "Crazy Place" and construct the staff.

Step 4:

Head to the centre of the map where the "Pack a Punch" is located and head down the stairwell where you picked up the music player. Go to the podium for the Ice Staff and construct your staff.
