Staff of Wind

Step 1:

The first step is to aquire the yellow musical disk which is located in the around Generator 5.

Step 2:

You then have to wait for the giant robot to pass through each section of the map. When you see the robot, head to that section and check both feet for a light. If you see the light under the foot, stand in one of its footprints that are on the same side as the foot that is lit up, and shoot the light just before the robot steps on you. This will teleport you into the head of the robot where one of three parts to the staff are held. Do this for each of the three robots and you will have all of the staff parts.

Step 3:

You will now have to get the Wind Crystal. This is located in the "Crazy Place". You must take the music player to the tunnel located at Generator 4 and place it down to open the portal to the crazy place. Walk through the portal and take the Wind Crystal from the podium. You can only carry one crystal at a time so you must now leave the "Crazy Place" and construct the staff.

Step 4:

Head to the centre of the map where the "Pack a Punch" is located and head down the stairwell where you picked up the music player. Go to the podium for the Wind Staff and construct your staff.
